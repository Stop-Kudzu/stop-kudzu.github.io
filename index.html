Stopping The Kudzu Invasion

KUDZU IS DESTROYING YOUR TREES. WILL YOU STOP IT?

What is Kudzu?

Kudzu is a vine that originally came from Asia but settled well into many ecosystems here in the USA. It lives by grabbing onto a larger plant, and using it to gain sunlight. In doing so, the Kudzu eventually smothers its host tree, killing it.

How To Stop It

Kudzu, though hardy, can be harvested using sharp implements. A problem with cutting kudzu is that it can regrow extremely fast, as much as one foot in a single day.
